omelette

Description

An omelette is a dish made from eggs, fried with butter or oil in a frying pan.

Ingredients

  • eggs
  • oil
  • salt
  • seasoning

Steps

  1. put an flat pan on the gas / stove
  2. in a bowl crack an egg and add some salt to taste ,mix well
  3. add some oil on the heating pan
  4. add the mixed eggs to the pan
  5. flip the omelette once in a min and serve it on the plate once both the sides are done
